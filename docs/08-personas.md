## The Three-Layer Agent Model

Each persona is not just demographics. It's three layers deep:

### Layer 1: The Soul (PersonaProfile)
Purchase psychology, price sensitivity, brand trust formation, social proof dependency, cultural context (language, festivals, family influence, city tier).

### Layer 2: The Memory
Past experiences shape future reactions. A persona who was burned by a brand remembers. See the **Memory System** for how this works.

### Layer 3: The Body (Action Space)
9 possible actions: SCROLL_PAST → PAUSE → READ → ENGAGE → CLICK → SAVE → SHARE → PURCHASE_INTENT → NEGATIVE_REACT

## Why Archetypes Matter

The **Critic Agent** validates that evaluations are **consistent with the archetype**. A Skeptic who gives a 95/100 with no objections gets quarantined. Archetypes are behavioral contracts.

## India-Specific Persona Design

- **City tier matters** — metro vs. tier-2 have fundamentally different trust signals
- **Language preference** — Hinglish, pure Hindi, English, regional
- **Family influence** — Indian purchases often involve family consensus
- **Festival psychology** — Diwali/Eid/Pongal create different buying modes
- **Platform behavior** — WhatsApp trust vs. Instagram discovery vs. YouTube research
